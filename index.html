#travis, sivart, sicxz

educator designer artist EWU Spokane, WA est. 2014

One person, three doors. The working record of an educator, a designer, and an artist.

My name is Travis Masingale. I am Sivart, aka sicxz. Travis is the educator, Sivart is the designer, sicxz is the artist. This site is the shell that lets all three speak in their own voice.
