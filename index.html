Travis Masingale.

Designer, educator, and applied-AI researcher. Full Professor of Visual Communication Design at Eastern Washington University. I work where code, design, and emergent practice meet — building tools, teaching the next class of designers, and helping institutions move from talking about AI to actually using it.
