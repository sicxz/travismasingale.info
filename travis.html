#travis — the educator

full professor EWU VCD since 2014 12 years

Twelve years of full-time teaching at Eastern Washington University. Courses, curriculum, governance, public talks. Source-level fluency as a teaching method.

educator designing art.

##what i teach

The teaching is built on source-level fluency. Students leave knowing what HTML and CSS actually are, what a design system is for, and how to read other people's work. The Brick-by-Brick method runs through every course.
